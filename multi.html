THE BIG 3

"The Big Three" typically refers to three dominant within a particular industry or field. In various contexts, this term has been used to describe leading companies, sports,teams or even groups of influential individuals.
For example, in the automotive industry, "The Big Three" often refers to General Motors, Ford and Chrysler, which have historically been the largest American car manufacturers.
In the context of anime, it usually denotes the trio of immensely popular series Naruto, Bleach and One Piece, that shaped the genre for a generation of fans.
Regardless of the field, "The Big Three" signifies power, influence and a benchmark for success, setting the standard for others to follow.
